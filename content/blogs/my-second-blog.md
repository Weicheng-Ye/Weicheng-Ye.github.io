This post collects links to open mathematical problems that I can find on the Internet.

<!--more-->

## 1. General indexes and cross-field collections

- [AIM Problem Lists](https://aimath.org/problemlists/) — The American Institute of Mathematics hosts workshop-generated lists covering many areas of pure and applied mathematics.
- [AMR Problem Lists](https://amathr.org/problems/) — The Association of Mathematics Research provides a field-by-field directory of classical and specialist open-problem collections.
- [UnsolvedMath](https://www.unsolvedmath.com/) — This searchable index aggregates more than two thousand questions from major notebooks and community collections.
- [FrontierMath Open Problems](https://epoch.ai/frontiermath/open-problems) — Epoch AI publishes expert-vetted research problems that can be filtered by field and status.
- [Open Problem Garden](https://www.openproblemgarden.org/) — This community-maintained wiki organizes problems by subject, but individual status claims should be independently checked.
- [MathWorld: Unsolved Problems](https://mathworld.wolfram.com/UnsolvedProblems.html) — MathWorld offers short introductions to many famous unsolved problems with references for further reading.
- [Clay Millennium Prize Problems](https://www.claymath.org/millennium-problems/) — The Clay Mathematics Institute maintains the official pages for the Millennium problems and their status.
- [Smale's Problems](https://www.fim.uni-passau.de/fileadmin/dokumente/fakultaeten/fim/lehrstuhl/muller/SmaleProblems1998.pdf) — Stephen Smale's 1998 list proposes eighteen mathematical problems for the twenty-first century.
- [Arnold's Problems](https://link.springer.com/book/10.1007/b138219) — This book gathers 861 problems posed by Vladimir Arnold over more than forty years, with historical comments and status information.
- [Open Problems in Mathematics](https://link.springer.com/book/10.1007/978-3-319-32162-2) — John Nash Jr. and Michael Th. Rassias edit a broad collection of expository chapters on major open directions.

## 2. Analysis

- [Seventy Five (Thousand) Unsolved Problems in Analysis and Partial Differential Equations](https://link.springer.com/article/10.1007/s00020-018-2460-8) — Vladimir Maz'ya surveys seventy-five problem clusters in function theory, functional analysis, and linear and nonlinear PDE.
- [Research Problems in Function Theory](https://arxiv.org/abs/1809.07200) — Walter Hayman and Eleanor Lingham assemble more than five hundred problems in complex analysis together with progress notes.
- [Some Problems in Harmonic Analysis](https://arxiv.org/abs/1701.06637) — This article records sixteen problems discussed at the 2016 AIM workshop on harmonic analysis.
- [Some Open Problems in Elasticity](https://people.maths.ox.ac.uk/ball/Articles%20in%20Conference%20Proceedings%20and%20Books/JMB%202002%20re%20Marsden%2060th.pdf) — John Ball lists foundational analytical questions in nonlinear elasticity and the calculus of variations.
- [Some Open Problems and Research Directions in the Mathematical Study of Fluid Dynamics](https://web.math.princeton.edu/~const/2k.pdf) — Peter Constantin sketches major analytical challenges in incompressible fluid dynamics.
- [Five Open Problems in Compressible Mathematical Fluid Dynamics](https://intlpress.com/site/pub/files/_fulltext/journals/maa/2013/0020/0002/MAA-2013-0020-0002-a006.pdf) — Denis Serre collects research questions about compressible flows, shocks, and conservation laws.
- [Stony Brook Open Problems in Dynamical Systems](https://www.math.stonybrook.edu/open-problems-dynamical-systems) — This portal gathers several conference and seminar problem lists in smooth and complex dynamics.
- [Fifteen Problems in Mathematical Physics](https://math.caltech.edu/SimonPapers/R27.pdf) — Barry Simon proposes fifteen problems spanning spectral theory, Schrödinger operators, and mathematical physics.
- [Michael Hochman's Open Problems in Dynamics](https://math.huji.ac.il/~mhochman/open-problems.html) — This regularly updated page lists problems in ergodic theory, entropy, fractals, and related dynamics.
- [Mike Boyle's Open Problems in Symbolic Dynamics](https://www.math.umd.edu/~mboyle/open/) — This page collects problem lists and updates centered on symbolic and topological dynamics.
- [Open Problems on Multiple Ergodic Averages](https://users.math.uoc.gr/~frantzikinakis/OpenProblems.html) — Nikos Frantzikinakis maintains questions on multiple recurrence, averages, and combinatorial applications.
- [Jesse Peterson's Open Problems](https://math.vanderbilt.edu/peters10/problems.html) — This maintained list focuses on operator algebras, measured group theory, and rigidity.
- [Open Problems in One-Parameter Operator Semigroups](https://arxiv.org/abs/2410.00416) — The survey collects research questions across semigroup theory, evolution equations, and operator theory.
- [Some Open Problems in Low-Dimensional Dynamical Systems](https://arxiv.org/abs/2012.02524) — Armengol Gasull presents thirty-three concrete problems about planar and low-dimensional dynamics.
- [Some Open Problems in Random Matrix Theory and the Theory of Integrable Systems](https://arxiv.org/abs/0712.0849) — Percy Deift connects open questions in random matrices, asymptotics, and integrable models.
- [Open Problems in Mathematical Physics](https://web.math.princeton.edu/~aizenman/OpenProblems_MathPhys/OPlist.html) — Michael Aizenman's page links a set of mathematically precise challenges motivated by physics.
- [Open Problems in Integrable Systems](https://pmc.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/articles/PMC6158379/) — This multi-author collection surveys open questions across classical, quantum, and discrete integrable systems.

## 3. Geometry

- [The Open Problems Project](https://topp.openproblem.net/) — This maintained database tracks dozens of problems in discrete and computational geometry, including status updates and references.
- [Open Problems in Geometry](https://academic.hep.com.cn/br/CN/chapter/9781571462411/chapter31) — Shing-Tung Yau's problem chapter ranges broadly across differential, complex, and algebraic geometry.
- [Differential Geometry: Its Past and Its Future](https://people.math.harvard.edu/~hirolee/pdfs/2014-fall-230a-icm1970-chern-differential-geometry.pdf) — Shiing-Shen Chern's 1970 address is a historical source of influential questions and research directions.
- [Problems from the Manifolds and Their Differential Geometry Conference](https://www.imo.universite-paris-saclay.fr/~pierre.pansu/problems_MTDG.pdf) — Frank Morgan and Pierre Pansu record concise problems contributed at a 2018 differential-geometry meeting.
- [101 Questions, Problems and Conjectures around Scalar Curvature](https://www.ihes.fr/~gromov/positivescalarcurvature/596/) — Mikhail Gromov organizes a large research agenda around scalar curvature and metric geometry.
- [Open Problems in Geometry of Curves and Surfaces](https://ghomi.math.gatech.edu/Papers/op.pdf) — Mohammad Ghomi collects approachable but research-level questions about curves, surfaces, convexity, and knots.
- [Questions in Differential Geometry](https://faculty.tcu.edu/richardson/DiffGeomQs.htm) — Ken Richardson curates links to problem lists in Riemannian, contact, symplectic, and related geometry.
- [Unsolved Problems in Geometry](https://link.springer.com/book/10.1007/978-1-4612-0963-8) — Croft, Falconer, and Guy present classical intuitive problems in convex, discrete, and combinatorial geometry.
- [Research Problems in Discrete Geometry](https://link.springer.com/book/10.1007/0-387-29929-7) — Brass, Moser, and Pach assemble an Erdős-style collection spanning incidence, packing, covering, and combinatorial geometry.
- [Some Open Problems in Sub-Riemannian Geometry](https://arxiv.org/abs/1304.2590) — Andrei Agrachev lists foundational questions about geodesics, regularity, and metric structure in sub-Riemannian spaces.
- [Some Open Problems in Holomorphic Symplectic Geometry](https://arxiv.org/abs/1002.4321) — Arnaud Beauville surveys conjectures and classification questions for irreducible holomorphic symplectic manifolds.
- [Open Problems in Symplectic Topology](https://academic.oup.com/book/43512/chapter/364196235) — McDuff and Salamon's book chapter provides a structured list of problems in symplectic geometry and topology.
- [Some Open Problems in Symplectic Topology](https://www.math.stonybrook.edu/events/dusafest/problems.pdf) — This compact conference handout records concrete questions about Hamiltonian actions, Hofer geometry, and Gromov–Witten theory.
- [Open Problems on Billiards and Quantitative Symplectic Geometry](https://arxiv.org/abs/2602.12896) — This recent survey links billiard dynamics with symplectic capacities and quantitative questions.
- [Geometry of Continued Fractions: Open Problems and Conjectures](https://arxiv.org/abs/1712.01450) — Oleg Karpenkov collects questions on multidimensional continued fractions and their geometric structures.
- [A Gallery of Open Problems in Geometry](https://arxiv.org/abs/2510.11908) — This illustrated collection presents a varied set of current geometric questions in a compact format.

## 4. Topology

- [Mark Hovey's Algebraic Topology Problem List](https://www-users.cse.umn.edu/~tlawson/hovey/) — This restored problem list organizes questions across homotopy theory, spectra, classifying spaces, and generalized cohomology.
- [Open Problems and Questions in Algebraic Geometry, Topology, and Differential Geometry](https://bookstore.ams.org/SURV/295) — This 2026 AMS volume centered on K3 surfaces contains nearly four hundred problems and many further subproblems.
- [Kirby's Problems in Low-Dimensional Topology](https://math.berkeley.edu/~kirby/) — Robion Kirby's classic list is a historical hub for problems about 3- and 4-manifolds, knots, and geometric topology.
- [Problems on Mapping Class Groups and Related Topics](https://math.uchicago.edu/~farb/papers/mcgbook.pdf) — Benson Farb's edited volume contains twenty-three chapters of problems linking topology, geometry, and dynamics.
- [Problems, Questions, and Conjectures about Mapping Class Groups](https://arxiv.org/abs/1806.08773) — Dan Margalit surveys a large set of questions about mapping class groups and their relatives.
- [Problems on Invariants of Knots and 3-Manifolds](https://arxiv.org/abs/math/0406190) — Ohtsuki and collaborators compile questions on quantum, finite-type, and classical invariants.
- [Virtual Knot Theory: Unsolved Problems](https://arxiv.org/abs/1409.2823) — This survey collects problems in virtual, combinatorial, and diagrammatic knot theory.
- [Problems in Foliations and Laminations of 3-Manifolds](https://arxiv.org/abs/math/0209081) — Danny Calegari lists research questions connecting foliations, laminations, contact structures, and group actions.
- [Problems in Groups, Geometry, and Three-Manifolds](https://arxiv.org/abs/1512.04620) — This workshop list gathers problems at the interface of geometric group theory and 3-manifold topology.
- [John Etnyre's Problems in Contact Topology](https://etnyre.math.gatech.edu/preprints/problems.html) — This page collects papers and lists devoted to open questions in contact topology.
- [Some Open Problems in Symplectic Topology](https://www.math.stonybrook.edu/~jasplund/open_problems.pdf) — This 2025 list emphasizes Floer homotopy theory and adjacent symplectic-topological questions.
- [Open Problems in Algebraic Topology and Homotopy Theory](https://mathoverflow.net/questions/135765/open-problems-in-algebraic-topology-and-homotopy-theory) — This MathOverflow thread is a community index of concrete questions and references in modern homotopy theory.
- [Wild Topology Problem List](https://wildtopology.com/problemlist/) — This specialist list focuses on wild embeddings, decomposition spaces, and pathological phenomena in topology.
- [Open Problems in Non-Hausdorff Topology and Domain Theory](https://topology.lmf.cnrs.fr/open-problems/) — This maintained site records numbered questions in non-Hausdorff topology, order theory, and theoretical computer science.
- [Open Problems in Arithmetic Topology](https://arxiv.org/abs/2012.15434) — This survey formulates analogies and questions linking knots, 3-manifolds, number fields, and arithmetic invariants.
- [Voevodsky's Open Problems in Motivic Stable Homotopy Theory](https://www.math.ias.edu/vladimir/node/56) — Vladimir Voevodsky's page states problems about the motivic stable homotopy category and its invariants.
- [Problems in the Topology of Manifolds](https://matrix-inst.org.au/wp_Matrix2016/wp-content/uploads/2019/01/Bowden.pdf) — This MATRIX workshop report compiles questions about manifold groups, embeddings, foliations, and geometric structures.
- [Open Problems in Topological Graph Theory](https://www.cambridge.org/core/books/abs/topics-in-topological-graph-theory/open-problems/A81C0F99E0A29C3A8985125D9B9B95AF) — Dan Archdeacon surveys questions about graph embeddings, genus, colorings, crossing numbers, and geometric representations.
- [Open Problems in Topology II: A Status Report](https://www.sciencedirect.com/science/article/pii/S0166864103001834) — This cumulative report tracks solutions and progress for the roughly eleven hundred problems in the classic topology volumes.

## 5. Algebra

- [The Kourovka Notebook](https://kourovkanotebookorg.wordpress.com/) — The long-running notebook's twenty-first edition collects new questions in group theory and updates many earlier entries.
- [The World of Groups: Open Problems](https://shpilrain.ccny.cuny.edu/gworld/problems/oproblems.html) — This directory links more than two hundred problems in combinatorial and computational group theory.
- [Questions in Geometric Group Theory](https://www.math.utah.edu/~bestvina/eprints/questions-updated.pdf) — Mladen Bestvina's influential list records problems on hyperbolic groups, boundaries, automorphisms, and group actions.
- [The Lyapin Problem Notebook](https://arxiv.org/abs/2604.04763) — This 2026 collection presents unsolved problems across several major directions in semigroup theory.
- [Open Problems in Commutative Ring Theory](https://www2.math.uconn.edu/~glaz/Publications_Selected%20Articles/OpenProblemsInCommutativeRingTheory.Springer14.pdf) — Sarah Glaz and collaborators assemble questions across homological, coherent, and non-Noetherian commutative algebra.
- [Further Problems in Commutative Algebra](https://www.cambridge.org/core/services/aop-cambridge-core/content/view/6955F8C5E3E1021B47BCB10F6488D296/9781107325517ans_p242-250_CBO.pdf/further_problems.pdf) — This appendix offers a concise set of research-level problems complementing a graduate text.
- [Open Problems on Central Simple Algebras](https://arxiv.org/abs/1006.3304) — A. S. Merkurjev's survey lists structural questions about division algebras, Brauer groups, and algebraic groups.
- [Open Problems in Hopf Algebras](https://www.birs.ca/workshops/2015/15w5053/report15w5053.pdf) — This BIRS workshop report records problems on classification, tensor categories, Nichols algebras, and quantum groups.
- [Open Problems in the Representation Theory of Artin Algebras](https://www.cambridge.org/core/books/abs/representation-theory-of-artin-algebras/open-problems/CAE51CA110167B64FA58D7F78DED6EF6) — Auslander, Reiten, and Smalø conclude their monograph with a focused list of representation-theoretic problems.
- [Open Problems in the Theory of Simple Modular Lie Algebras](https://arxiv.org/abs/1503.06762) — Georgia Benkart, Thomas Gregory, and Alexander Premet survey classification and representation questions in positive characteristic.
- [nLab: Open Problems in Category Theory](https://ncatlab.org/nlab/show/open%20problems%20in%20category%20theory) — The nLab community maintains a cross-linked collection of conjectures and questions in category and higher-category theory.
- [Open Problems in Operad Theory](https://doi.org/10.1142/9789814365123_0007) — This chapter gathers questions about operads, homotopy algebras, deformation theory, and applications.
- [Open Problems on Open Algebraic Varieties](https://arxiv.org/abs/alg-geom/9506006) — Shulim Kaliman and Mikhail Zaidenberg survey questions concerning affine and open complex algebraic varieties.
- [Algebraic Stories from One and from the Other Pockets](https://amj.math.stonybrook.edu/pdf-Springer-final/018-0088.pdf) — Vladimir Shpilrain's essay presents accessible open problems across combinatorial and computational algebra.
- [The Finite Lattice Representation Problem](https://universalalgebra.org/) — This site explains the history, current status, partial results, and related questions surrounding a central problem in universal algebra.
- [Open Problems in Representation Theory and Around](https://mathoverflow.net/questions/96202/open-problems-questions-in-representation-theory-and-around/) — This MathOverflow thread gathers community-contributed questions and references across representation theory.
- [Open Problems in Hopf Algebras on MathOverflow](https://mathoverflow.net/questions/231160/open-problems-in-hopf-algebras) — This community thread complements formal surveys with specific Hopf-algebra questions and literature pointers.
- [Open Problems on Pro-Lie Groups](https://arxiv.org/abs/1506.06852) — Hofmann and Morris list questions about infinite-dimensional Lie theory and projective limits of Lie groups.
- [Selected Open Problems in Topological Algebra](https://arxiv.org/abs/1202.1619) — Dikran Dikranjan and Michael Megrelishvili survey problems involving topological groups, compactifications, and dynamics.
